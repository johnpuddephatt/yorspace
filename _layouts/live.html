Housing with affordability at its heart

Unlike most houses, the homes we’re creating won’t ever be sold on the open market forquick profit.

Instead they’ll be owned by a cooperative which residents will be shareholding members of, paying a deposit plus monthly contributions which give you security and equity while avoiding the inflation and speculation of traditional home ownership.
